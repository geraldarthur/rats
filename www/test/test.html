Eek, a Rat!

Help us locate areas of infestation.

by Gerald Rich

August 12, 2013 9:00 AM

Last year 311 received more calls related to rodent activity than in years past.

This map shows concentrations of those reports. Although more reports do not mean more rats, the city has said that more reporting can help them better assess the infestation.
